Instructions for Keyboard Gameplay

×
  1. Starting a New Game:
    • Press the Enter key to start a new game.
  2. Navigation:
    • Use the arrow keys (Up, Down, Left, Right) to navigate the grid.
    • Move the cursor to the desired cell.
  3. Revealing Cells:
    • Press the 'c' key to reveal the content of the highlighted cell.
    • Be cautious! Revealing a mine will end the game.
  4. Flagging Mines:
    • Press the 'w' key to flag/unflag a suspected mine.
    • Use flags to mark cells that you believe contain mines.
  5. Double Click Action:
    • Double-click on a cell to reveal adjacent cells if the neighboring mines are correctly flagged around it.
    • Press the 'x' reveal around the highlighted cell.
  6. Winning the Game:
    • Successfully reveal all non-mine cells to win the game.
    • Use logic and strategy to uncover safe cells without triggering mines.
